Ghost Pepper Steak With Haunted Bourbon Cream Sauce

A spicy steak for heat and meat lovers!

You have to like hot stuff to enjoy these steaks. The sauce is absolutely delish! This is a quick main course that goes great with any sides

Ingredients

  • 2 rib-eye steaks
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 ghost pepper finely chopped adjust for preferred heat
  • 1/2 cup bourbon
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder

Steps

  1. Season steaks with salt and pepper
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet and cook steaks for 4-5 minutes per side for medium rare.
  3. Remove steaks from the skillet, melt butter, and saute ghost pepper for 1 minute
  4. Add bourbon and simmer for 3-4 minutes.
  5. Stir in heavy cream and garlic powder. Cook until thickened.
  6. Pour the haunted bourbon cream sauce over the steaks and serve with your favorite sides
