A southern bean salad that makes a great dip

This dip is great for any gathering. It goes well with about any main course and is loved by all. A great combination of beans, and vegetables, with a delicious splash of juicy flavor!

Ingredients

  • 3 Roma tomatoes seeds removed, diced
  • 2 Avocados diced
  • 1/3 cup diced red onion
  • 15 ounces canned black beans rinsed and drained
  • 15 onces canned black eyed peas rinsed and drained
  • 1 1/2 cups frozen corn kernels thawed
  • 1 bell pepperdiced, any color
  • 1 jalapeno seeds removed, finely diced
  • 1/3 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • tortilla chipsfor serving

Dressing

  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

Steps

  1. Combine tomatows, avacado, onion, black beans, black eyed peas, corn, pepper, jalapeno pepper, and cilantro in a large bowl. Toss/stir well so that ingredients are well combined.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together olive oil, lime juice, red wine vinegar, sugar, salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  3. Pour dressing over the other ingredients and stir/toss very well.
  4. Keep refrigerated. If not serving immediately, be sure to toss/stir well before serving.
